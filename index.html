Introduction

Hired as an Analytics Consultant by Citi Bike Data to determine Citi Bike usage patterns during the week and identify opportunities to increase ridership. This comprehensive analysis provides insights into usage trends based on customer type, seasonality, and geographic distribution. The findings support a strategic roadmap for expanding the customer base and identifying potential new locations for growth.

Business Questions

  1. What type of customer should we target, and what are their preferences?
  2. What are the most popular pick-up locations across the city for Citi Bike rental?
  3. What types of bikes should we offer?
  4. How can we align prices with customer value perception?

About the Data

The dataset includes Citi Bike rentals from 2019 and 2020, combined into one dataset. The data consists of one table with 14 fields and 288,443 records in CSV format.

Insights

Locations

The top 10 stations were primarily located in Jersey City, with fewer in Hoboken and New York.

Bike Use

March was the most popular month for biking, likely due to favorable weather. Usage was steady throughout the week, with an increase observed over the weekends.

Recommendations

Target Customers

  • Urban commuters seeking convenience and time efficiency.
  • Tourists exploring the city flexibly.
  • Fitness-conscious users looking for eco-friendly transportation.

Customer Attraction Strategies

  • Easy access via mobile apps or kiosks.
  • Affordable, flexible pricing (pay-per-ride or subscription).
  • Variety of bikes (standard, electric, specialty).
  • Emphasis on safety and bike maintenance.

Location Strategy

Focus on high-traffic areas such as public transit hubs, downtown business districts, tourist hotspots, universities, parks, and recreational zones.

Bike Types to Offer

  • Standard Bikes: Low-cost, reliable, ideal for short urban trips.
  • Electric Bikes (E-Bikes): Great for longer or hilly rides; appeals to premium users.
  • Specialty Bikes: Tandem or cargo bikes for tourism or delivery purposes.
  • Features: Adjustable seats, baskets, GPS-enabled locks.

Pricing Recommendations

  • Offer tiered pricing: pay-per-ride, monthly, or yearly subscriptions.
  • Dynamic pricing for high-demand areas or times.
  • Highlight value through convenience, speed, and sustainability.

Data Limitations

Unable to distinguish between male and female rider usage due to missing demographic fields.
